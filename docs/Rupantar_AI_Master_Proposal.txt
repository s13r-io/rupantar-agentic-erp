Part 1 — Executive Summary


India has approximately 50 million micro and small businesses — kiranas, restaurants, pharmacies, garages,
salons, fashion shops, coaching centres, and more. They collectively handle ₹95 lakh crore in annual retail
and services. Yet 80–85% of them use no operational software at all. The ones that do are underserved by
legacy desktop tools that were designed for accountants, not shopkeepers.

The opportunity is not incremental. It is a category creation: moving India's unorganised trade from paper and
memory to AI-native operations — delivered through WhatsApp, configurable without code, and adopted
without requiring any behavioral change from the shop owner.

    •   Offline Sync: Queues all transactions during internet outage, syncs with conflict resolution when
