# Rupantar AI — Executive Brief

## AI-Powered Operational Platform for India's Retail and Services Economy

**March 2026 | Confidential**

---

## 1. What We're Proposing

Rupantar AI is building an AI-powered operational platform for India's retail and services economy — delivered through WhatsApp and voice, deployed by India's existing field force, and monetised through SaaS subscriptions that are profitable from the first shop. We build two things: (1) AI agent bundles that run shopkeeper operations on WhatsApp (billing, inventory, credit, procurement) — revenue from SaaS subscriptions at **₹299–₹1,499**/month; (2) Agent Builder, a no-code platform that lets trained partners deploy agent bundles in under **60 minutes** — revenue from platform fees and marketplace royalties. We distribute through India's existing field sales force (FMCG reps, pharma MRs, Tally resellers) trained as certified **AI Shop Doctors** who earn recurring income from every active shop.

## 3. What We Build

**The Product.** AI agents on WhatsApp and voice. Each shop gets **3–5** vertical-specific agents. Shopkeepers use voice notes, photos, and one-word replies — zero conventional UI. **20** verticals from kirana to pharmacy to services.

**The Platform.** Agent Builder is a **four-layer** tool (library, visual flow builder, config studio, pro-code SDK). A non-developer partner deploys a bundle in **<60 minutes**. Without it, every shop would need an engineer.

| | What | Revenue |
|---|---|---|
| **Product** | AI agents on WhatsApp/voice | SaaS subscriptions per shop |
| **Platform** | Agent Builder (4-layer tool) | Platform fees + marketplace royalties |
| **Distribution** | AI Shop Doctor network | Enables product sales (S&M cost) |

---

## 4. How We Distribute It

We are not building a greenfield sales force. We convert India's existing **10M+** field force — FMCG, pharma, software resellers — into certified AI Shop Doctors. Tally proved this model for **30 years**: ~50K TDPs generated **₹900 Cr** revenue for the ecosystem. Same motion: train, certify, recurring attach per shop.

Partners earn **15–30%** recurring commission plus activation fees and customisation fees.

## 7. Why Now

LLM-based agents have crossed the accuracy bar for Indian vernacular commercial language at usable cost. WhatsApp is already the default channel for Indian SMB owners — **50M+** business accounts. Incumbents have not occupied this wedge: Tally remains desktop-centric, Vyapar is app-first, Khatabook did not convert usage to durable monetisation. Category creation in agentic, zero-UI shop ops is open.

## 8. Risk Awareness

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Voice accuracy in noisy shops | Phase 0: **50** shops, **>85%** target; compounds with data |
| WhatsApp/Meta dependency | Multi-channel fallback (voice-only, SMS); contractual protections |
| Partner attrition | Income tracking from day 1; **80%+** must earn **>₹15K** by month 3 |
| Competitive entry | **18-month** head start in partners + vernacular data + templates |
| Data governance (DPDP) | Privacy-by-design; shopkeeper owns data; Rupantar is fiduciary |
